Media Ecosystem Observatory

Junior Data Analyst

During my contract at MEO, I collaborated with a multidisciplinary team of data analysts to build a first-of-its-kind seedlist of political entities on social media. Due to the absence of public APIs and increasing platform-level data restrictions, this work relied on creative collection strategies, including network-based discovery and manual identification. Originally focused on Canadian political actors, the seedlist has since expanded to U.S.-based entities, where restricted data access has created significant gaps in independent research capacity. The seedlist has since enabled numerous published reports and remains central to ongoing research on the North American information ecosystem.

I also led a mixed-methods analysis of political engagement on Bluesky, examining how political entities are using the platform and how its communicative dynamics differ from those of established social media platforms.
